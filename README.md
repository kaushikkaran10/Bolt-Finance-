# Bolt Finance ⚡

**Bolt Finance** is an AI-powered ecosystem designed to bridge the gap between financial literacy and real-world market intelligence. This full-stack platform integrates education, paper trading, and AI-driven advisory into a single, cohesive experience.

---

## ✨ Key Features

* **🎓 Learning Hub:** Structured financial modules with assessments and progress tracking.
* **📈 Prediction Playground:** A paper-trading arena where you test your intuition against ML-generated market predictions.
* **📰 News Intelligence:** Live analysis of financial news with visual mapping of sentiment impact on market movements.
* **📊 Portfolio Analyzer:** A comprehensive dashboard providing insights into asset allocation, performance, and risk.
* **🤖 AI Strategy Advisor:** A conversational chatbot that offers personalized strategy suggestions based on real-time news and portfolio data.
